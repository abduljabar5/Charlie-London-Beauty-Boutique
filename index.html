About Me

Hello,
My name is Charlie London. I am a hairstylist in love with the art of bringing people visions to life. From moms on the go to fashion model. I love to create looks that will work alongside a woman’s natural beauty. Every thing from music videos, runway shows to creative photo shoots it’s my greatest hope that you think of me when considering a stylist that can create the best look possible. “I am a woman before I am a hairstylist”. Is my slogan so trust me when I say I understand the importance between being unique and original but true to your inner self.
